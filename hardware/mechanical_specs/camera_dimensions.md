| PCB height | 24 mm |

## Bracket Clearances

- Minimum bracket inner width: **26 mm** (1 mm side clearance each)
- Lens aperture cutout: **9 mm diameter** minimum
- Ribbon cable slot: **17 mm × 3 mm**
